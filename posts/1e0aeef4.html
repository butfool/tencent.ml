经验篇 | 开启网络共享后仍无法找到计算机

发表于 2020-08-26 更新于 2020-08-27 分类于 其他 ， 杂篇 阅读次数： 本文字数： 390 阅读时长 ≈ 1 分钟
The computer in the LAN cannot be found because the related service is not open.

Reference:

开启网络共享后在网络中找不到此计算机

Problem description

Today at home, I tried to transfer a large file between two computers using a local area network. However, when I opened the “network” in resource manager, I found nothing.

All the jobs mentioned on the Internet had been done, but it still can’t work well.

Solution

Open the “service” window, and find the”Function Discovery Resource Publication”service. Launch or relaunch it.

Launch the service related to it
